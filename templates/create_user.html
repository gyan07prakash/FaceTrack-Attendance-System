🙋‍♂️ How to Use FaceLog

FaceLog is a facial recognition-based system that helps you mark attendance and track time automatically using your face. Here's how to get started:

👤 1. Add a New User
Click on “Add User” from the dashboard.
Enter your name and let the webcam capture your face from different angles.
Once saved, your face data will be stored securely.

🧠 2. Train the Model
After adding users, click on “Train Model.”
The system will learn and recognize the faces you've registered.
This step is only needed after adding or deleting users.

🕒 3. Mark Your Attendance
✅ Check-in: Click “Check-in” to start your work session.
❌ Check-out: Click “Check-out” at the end of your session.
Your time and duration will be recorded automatically.

📅 4. View Attendance
Click “View Attendance” to see the full log of names, dates, times, and total work hours.

🧑‍💼 5. Manage Users
Use “Manage Users” to view or delete registered users.
